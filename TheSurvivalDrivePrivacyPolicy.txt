Privacy Policy for Google Play

Effective Date: 14.05.2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Google transmits, uses, and discloses information obtained from users when using Google Play services. We adhere to principles of protecting your privacy and strive to ensure the proper protection of your personal data. Please carefully read our Privacy Policy to fully understand how we process your information.

1. Information Transmitted
1.1 Device Information: We may automatically transmit information about your device, including the model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

1.2 Usage Information: We may transmit information about your use of Google Play services, such as app usage, interactions with content, and preferences.

1.3 Location Information: With your consent, we may transmit and process information about your device's precise location. This helps us provide location-based services and enhance your user experience.

2. Use of Transmitted Information
2.1 Providing and Improving Services: We use the information transmitted to us to provide, maintain, and improve Google Play services, including personalized recommendations, app updates, and troubleshooting.

2.2 Personalization: We may use the information to personalize your experience on Google Play, including displaying relevant content, recommendations, and tailored advertisements.

2.3 Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, updates, and promotions related to Google Play services. You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications at any time.

2.4 Analytics and Research: We may aggregate and anonymize the information transmitted to us for analytical purposes, such as understanding user trends, improving our services, and conducting research.

3. Information Sharing
3.1 Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering and improving Google Play services. These providers are obligated to handle your information securely and in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

3.2 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request. We may also enforce our policies and investigate potential violations.

3.3 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity or successor.

4. Data Security
We implement security measures to protect the information transmitted to us from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices
5.1 Location Information: You can enable or disable location services for Google Play through your device settings or app permissions.

5.2 Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized advertisements and promotional communications by adjusting your preferences in the Google Play settings or following the instructions provided in the communication.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised policy will become effective when published. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
